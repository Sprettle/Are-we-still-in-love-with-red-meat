Are Australian's still in love with

RED MEAT ?

Australia has a long history as a country that loves its red meat. Meat production and processing in Australia occupies over half of the land mass and makes an important contribution to the Australian economy.

Meat also has cultural and social significance, as seen through many advertising campaigns promoting meat of all types over the years. Such advertisements include “Red Meat Amazing Food”, Australia Day Lambassador Sam Kekovic and the promotion of "Get some pork on your fork" just to name a few.

Is the act of “throwing a steak on the barbie” as popular as it once was? Have our tastes changed as the demographic of our population has changed?

So ask yourself Are Australian’s still in love with RED MEAT?
