What Does the Future Hold For Red Meat?

Can you imagine a future where only insect meat is eaten? Or a future where all of the meat we eat is grown in a lab? Or a future where there is no meat, only vegetable based products?

"Humans have no need for animal products, and the increasing number of vegans on the planet is a testament to how easy it is to live a healthy, fulfilling life without participating in the systematic abuse and unnecessary killing that goes on in the animal industry every day." - Angel Flinn

Vegans, Vegetarians, and others who follow a plant-based diet continue to grow. Whilst the availability of data is limited on how many people in Australia are eating all or almost all plant-based diets, what data is available shows a steady increase.
